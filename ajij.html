Samosa

A crispy, triangular Indian snack filled with spiced potatoes, peas, or meat. It's deep-fried to golden perfection and often served with chutneys. Popular as a street food and party appetizer, its irresistible flavor and flaky crust make it a global favorite.

Egg

A versatile superfood, eggs are packed with protein, vitamins, and minerals. They can be boiled, fried, scrambled, or baked. Essential in baking and cooking, eggs are a dietary staple worldwide, symbolizing nourishment and life.

Tandoori

Tandoori is a traditional Indian cooking method using a clay oven, or tandoor. Foods like chicken, paneer, and naan are marinated in spices and yogurt, then roasted, producing smoky, flavorful dishes with vibrant red hues, celebrated globally.
